내가 좋아하는 한국의 산 TOP 5

1. 지리산(천왕봉, 해발 1915m)

지리산은 지리산은 경상남도, 전라남도, 전북특별자치도에 걸쳐 있는 산이다. 주봉인 천왕봉 외에 반야봉, 연화봉, 바래봉을 비롯하여 뱀사골, 피아골, 한신계곡이 유명하다. 지리산은 사계절 모두 아름다워 연간 300만명이 찾는다.

K2

K2 is the second-highest mountain in the world, located on the China–Pakistan border. It is known for its extreme difficulty and has the second-highest fatality rate among the eight-thousanders.

Kangchenjunga

Kangchenjunga is the third-highest mountain in the world. It lies between Nepal and Sikkim, India, with three of the five peaks directly on the border, and the remaining two in Nepal's Taplejung District.

Lhotse

Lhotse is the fourth-highest mountain in the world, located at the border between Tibet (China) and the Khumbu region of Nepal. It is connected to Mount Everest via the South Col.

Makalu

Makalu is the fifth-highest mountain in the world, located in the Mahalangur range of the Nepal Himalayas, southeast of Mount Everest. It is an isolated peak whose shape is a four-sided pyramid.
